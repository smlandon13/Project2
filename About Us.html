About Us

Zoom is an an MTC delivery and fulfillment service that started in Utah County. Here at Zoom we aim to provide a service for friends and families of missionaries currently at the Provo Missionary Training Center. No matter where you are in the United States, through Zoom, you can send your missionary a care package that we deliver the very next day. Select any of the products on our site and we will package it and deliver it directly to the MTC!
